About

The artist formerly known as Daniel Patrick Ellis is an American recording artist, actor and model.

Ellis first came to prominence as a solo artist on Radio Disney and subsequently toured with Miley Cyrus and the Jonas Brothers.

Following a series of stints on TV and in movies including CSI, Gossip Girl and Bad Santa, he has since taken a hiatus to complete a degree in broadcast journalism at the University of Miami.

Currently, Dan is recording new music with international producer and DJ Tracy Young. He has left his squeaky-clean image behind for an edgier look that coincides with his new music which will be released in 2019.
